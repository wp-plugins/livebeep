== Installation ==

1. First, download and unzip the **livebeep-chat.zip** to your WordPress **`/wp-content/plugins/`** directory.
2. Then, **activate** the LiveBeep plugin through the **'Plugins' menu** in WordPress.
3. Finally, choose the **LiveBeep Chat** link to complete registration.
4. You are done! Once registered, the widget is created instantly in your website. Enjoy all the features included in LiveBeep!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to install any software on my computer? =
No. LiveBeep is a Saas service (Software as a Service). The whole software and associated data are centrally hosted on the cloud.

= Can I customize chat/e-mail invitations? =
Yes, you can set your corporate colors and logo not only in the invitations, but also in the whole promotional and communication material generated by LiveBeep: web ads, multimedia contents, commercial e-mails, etc.

= Do I have to be online in order to attract new customers? =
Not necessarily. When you are online and Available (online status), visitors can chat with you. When you are offline or Not available (online status), visitors can contact through e-mail. LiveBeep invitations work 24/7. 

=Can I be logged in but appear as not available for chat? =
Yes, you just turn your online status to Not available.

= How do I know if I receive an e-mail through LiveBeep? =
Every e-mail contact generates a notification on your personal e-mail address so you can answer it in real time from a PC, tablet or smartphone. You don't have to be logged in to answer to your visitors.

= Is it possible to manage different domains from the same account? =
Absolutely. There are no restrictions on the number of active domains.

= Can I deactivate proactive invitations on my website momentarily without removing the plugin or closing my LiveBeep account? =
Yes, you can activate/deactivate invitations whenever you want. You can also set specific operating hours for invitations.

= Can I synchronize contacts info generated by LiveBeep with external CRMs?=
Yes, you can synchronize your CRM, though you should do it through a GET request.

= Why should I choose LiveBeep instead of other live chat solutions? =
LiveBeep is much more than a live chat. On the [Description](http://www.wordpress.org/plugins/livebeep/ "Description") of our plugin, you will find the main features of LiveBeep platform. Or just visit [http://livebeep.com](http://www.LiveBeep.com/ "http://livebeep.com") for more information. 

= Is it free to use? =
The LiveBeep plugin comes with a free **limited** account designed for WordPress users. If your business needs the full service, you can upgrade your account anytime.

= Something goes wrong! LiveBeep is not working on my site! =
We will be happy to give you assistance! Visit [http://livebeep.com](http://www.LiveBeep.com/ "http://livebeep.com") or send us an email at support@livebeep.com and we will guide you through the whole installation process.

= I tried LiveBeep and it is not what I needed. How can I remove LiveBeep from my site? =
You have to simply remove LiveBeep plugin from your WordPress dashboard.
